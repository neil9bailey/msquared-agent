# Incident Escalation

Escalate to a human owner when:

- A draft receives `block` risk.
- The source asks legal, compliance, security, contractual, press, or analyst questions.
- A post or email could disclose private customer facts or decision-pack content.
- A connector fails repeatedly.
- X or mailbox behavior appears abusive, spam-like, or outside the approved use case.

Default action: reject or rewrite the draft, preserve the audit trail, and do not post or send.
