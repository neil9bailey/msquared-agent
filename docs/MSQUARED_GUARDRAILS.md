# MSquared Guardrails

- All public-facing content is draft-first.
- Human approval is mandatory before posting, replying, DMing, or sending email.
- No autonomous approval claims.
- No compliance certification claims.
- No claim that M2 proves truth, reads all upstream internals, or knows model intent.
- No fake engagement, bot swarms, duplicate posts, trend manipulation, unsolicited DMs, or keyword-search auto-replies.
- No private customer facts or decision-pack content in public posts.
- Maintain audit records for drafted, approved, rejected, sent, posted, and blocked actions.
- Respect opt-out records before outbound DM/email automation.
- Keep learning metadata-only unless separately approved.
